Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

  1. The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of the city.
  2. Visit the Louvre Museum to see thousands of works of art.
  3. Explore the historic *Notre-Dame Cathedral*.
  4. Walk along the Champs-Élysées avenue.
  5. See a show at the Moulin Rouge cabaret.
  • Try some delicious croissants and pain au chocolat.
  • Savor the classic French dish, *escargots*.
  • Enjoy a variety of cheeses like Camembert and Brie.
  • Indulge in colorful *macarons* from local patisseries.
  • A warm bowl of French onion soup is a must.
