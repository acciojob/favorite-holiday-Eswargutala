Paris


Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Eiffel Tower: An iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. The Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. The Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. The Arc de Triomphe: Honors those who fought and died for France in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars.
  5. The Musée d'Orsay: Houses mostly French art dating from 1848 to 1914.

Must-Try Foods

  • A fresh croissant from a local boulangerie.
  • Authentic French baguettes.
  • Macarons from Laudrée or Pierre Hermé.
  • Escargots (snails) for the adventurous.
  • Delicious French cheese platters.
